New Homes

The Fisher Brothers Electrical Contractors New Homes division offers a team of highly experienced, skilled and trained professionals whose primary focus is to deliver a full and complete range of electrical services to domestic builders and property owners.

Fisher Brothers Electrical Contractors New Homes division can provide solutions for all types of electrical needs from the ground up. We can meet all requirements from underground power supply, power and lighting systems, alarm systems, intercoms, CCTV and home automation in the domestic sector.

From the owner builder to small, medium and large volume builders we have a trusted team of experienced consultants and tradesman who will deliver on each project.

We ensure that we tailor our services to suite all our customers’ needs and requirements and guarantee complete customer satisfaction.

All Fisher Brothers Electrical Contractors new homes are delivered with the highest standards and are strictly in line with all industry guidelines and standards.
